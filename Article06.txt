Etoro: The Social Trading Platform founded by brothers Yoni and Ronen Assia

## Post ##
Etoro is a social trading and multi-asset brokerage company that was founded in 2007 by two brothers, Yoni Assia and Ronen Assia. The company is based in Tel Aviv, Israel and has offices in Cyprus, the United Kingdom, and Australia.

Etoro offers its clients a platform that allows them to trade various financial instruments such as stocks, currencies, commodities, and indices. The platform also allows users to copy the trades of other successful traders in the community, which is one of the main features that sets it apart from traditional online brokerages.

Etoro's mission is to open the financial markets to everyone, and make it easy for people to trade and invest in a variety of assets. The company's vision is to create a world where anyone can trade, invest and save, regardless of their level of expertise.

Etoro has grown rapidly since its founding and has over 20 million registered users in over 140 countries. The company is regulated by the Cyprus Securities and Exchange Commission (CySEC) and the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) in the United Kingdom.
